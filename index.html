Hello!

My name is Aspen Kesler. I’m a freelance front-end web developer through my company: Kesler Digital Expressions. I started learning web development through an early-college program during my senior year of high school and I'm continuing to teach myself new languages and programs all the time. I’m fluent in multiple web languages and certified in many Adobe programs.

When I’m not building websites or editing videos, I’m actively pursuing a career in the field of Interior Architecture and Design. I’ve loved exploring these two career paths and finding the similarities between them. I’m always looking for ways to be creative, help others, and expand my skill set.

Navigate to my Contact Me page if you are interested in working with me or have any questions.

Portfolio

During my internship, I was tasked with integrating a related website that was built on Weebly.com to the main website built on WordPress.com. As apart of the transfer, I built a custom-coded menu bar to replicate the feeling of an external site within this company's main website. Link to website below.

It was a pleasure to work with such an influential company and this project gave me the opportunity to explore GoDaddy's Website Builder and Hosting Service in depth. This website has since been taken over by another developer.
